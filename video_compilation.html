The Big Three of Anime

The Big Three of Anime consists of three major anime which are Naruto, One Piece and Bleach

Naruto

Naruto is one of the most watched anime of all time and is part of the big three.

One Piece

One Piece has the most sold manga of all time and is currently airing.

Bleach

Bleach has the best art-style out of the three and has inspired many shonen new gen anime.
